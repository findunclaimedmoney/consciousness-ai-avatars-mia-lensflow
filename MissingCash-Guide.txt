#####################################################
PART C — AD TRACKING (HOW TO KNOW WHAT'S WORKING)
#####################################################

Add a tag to the end of your link like this:

    https://YOURSITE/start?v=cars1

The "?v=cars1" part is the tag. It travels WITH the link wherever you
put it — video ad, post, bio link, email, banner. Whoever clicks that
exact link gets counted under that tag, and the tag shows up in the
lead email so you know where it came from.

GIVE EACH PLACE ITS OWN TAG:
    Video ad #1   ->  https://YOURSITE/start?v=cars1
    Video ad #2   ->  https://YOURSITE/start?v=cars2
    A post        ->  https://YOURSITE/start?v=post1
    Link in bio   ->  https://YOURSITE/start?v=bio
(You can name tags anything short with no spaces: ?v=super1, etc.)

THE ONLY RULE:
 - Full tagged link clicked -> lead shows the tag.
 - Plain address with no tag -> lead still arrives, just no tag.

TIKTOK TIP (videos vs posts):
 - Organic POSTS: TikTok usually won't let people tap a caption link,
   so put your tagged link in your BIO.
 - ADS: get a tappable button — point it straight at your tagged link.
 Both capture the tag fine; only the link's location differs.

PART F — THE ONE JOB LEFT: VERIFY missingcash.com.au
#####################################################

Doing this lets your leads come from YOUR OWN branded address and lets
Erin be CC'd automatically. Until it's done, everything still works —
leads just come from the lensflow address to you only.

STEP-BY-STEP:

 1. Log in to Resend (https://resend.com).

 2. Go to "Domains" in the left menu, click "Add Domain", and enter:
        missingcash.com.au

 3. Resend will show you a list of DNS records (these look like a few
    rows of DKIM / SPF / sometimes DMARC entries). Keep that page open.

 4. Log in to wherever your domain's DNS is managed (for missingcash
    this is Cloudflare). Add each record EXACTLY as Resend shows it
    (copy/paste the name and value for each one).

 5. Go back to Resend and wait for the domain to show "Verified".
    This can take a few minutes to a few hours after the DNS records
    are added.

 6. Tell me once it shows "Verified". I then flip ONE setting
    (MISSINGCASH_DOMAIN_VERIFIED = true) and re-publish.

 ** If any of the DNS step feels fiddly, just send me a screenshot of
    the records Resend gives you and I'll tell you exactly what to put
    where. **
