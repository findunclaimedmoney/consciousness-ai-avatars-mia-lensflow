# 🎙️ VOICEOVER TIPS

✅ Speak slowly and clearly
✅ Pause after big numbers
✅ Emphasise words like FREE, BILLION, YOUR MONEY
✅ Sound curious not salesy
✅ Record in a quiet room
✅ Use your normal Australian accent — it builds trust

❌ Don't rush through it
❌ Don't sound like you're reading
❌ Don't use a fake American accent
❌ Don't add too much music — voice must be clear
